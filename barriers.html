Barriers & Obstacles

Common barriers to food security — and ways to overcome them.

Poverty & Inequality

Poverty and inequality are root causes of hunger. Breaking the cycle of deprivation (education, housing, jobs) is critical (World Food Program USA, 2022).

Economic Shocks

Events like COVID‑19 reduced incomes and disrupted supply chains. Investing in sustainable food systems builds resilience (USGLC, n.d.).

Eligibility Restrictions

Programs like SNAP exclude some families. Revising guidelines and raising benefits helps (CLASP, 2025).

Environmental Degradation

Floods and droughts damage agriculture. Regenerative farming supports climate and equity (NRDC, 2021).
